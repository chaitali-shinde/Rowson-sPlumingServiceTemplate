FAQs

Are you a fully licensed plumber?

Yes, we are a fully licensed Perth plumber to carry out all plumbing, drainage, and gas plumbing jobs. This license allows us to do everything from roofing work as well as electrical wiring for hot water systems - but it also lets you know that we're the people who can fix your clogged drains or blocked pipes quickly and efficiently!

What areas do you service?

We service all areas within the Perth region.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes, we offer warranties on all installations and repairs to ensure your peace of mind.

How can I prevent my drains from clogging and smelling bad?

Regular maintenance and careful disposal of waste can help prevent clogs and odors.

Can I install a Hot Water system on my own?

No, it's highly recommended that a licensed professional handle hot water system installations for safety and warranty purposes.
